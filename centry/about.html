about the company

centry oy

was formed on January 1, 2016, with the merger between Oy Temigroup Nordic Ab and Logisec Oy, both companies registered in Finland.

The purpose of the merger is to provide your organization with comprehensive services relating to Security Risk Management Services and Compliance and Investigative Services.

The Company objectives are to work together with your organization in enabling your operations anywhere while providing

best practices in safeguarding your people, including personnel and all other stakeholders, property, both physical and intellectual, and the surroundings where you work.

Oy Temigroup Nordic Ab

was formed in 2009, a founding member of Temigroup International. Temigroup Nordic became a center point of a global network of security practitioners, collaborating on services and projects to provide superior results around the world.

Logisec Oy

was formed in 2009, a company which specializing in security training, auditing and consulting, with core emphasis on supply chain; from the manufacturer,forwarding and transportation, through forwarding and transportation,all the way to the end customer.By time of the merge, Logisec had become a leader in its market sector.

Centry Global office locations are Finland (HQ), Canada, Thailand, Poland and Ukraine.
